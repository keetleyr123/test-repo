What you can do to make a difference in 2022

We told you all about what we do in order to help our kittens feel safe and protected as well as loved. Now we need to know what it is that you will do to help these kittens find a home. Adopt Today!


Kittens need all the love and attention they can get so what are you waiting for?! Adopt today and be the difference in your community and to these loving animals!


We will provide all the shots and food to get you started as well as make sure there is nothing wrong with your kitten and we also will include a free toy as well!


If you know of any other friends or family that are willing to adopt spread the word and let them know! also feel free to let us know what you think!
