Inside house cats are fun and unique and tend to live longer than outdoor cats. Inside cats are easy to maintain and will cost you less in the long run.

Outdoor Cats

Cats that are free to roam outside and enjoy life as they seek fit. These types of cats are more suceptible to getting infections, fleas, worms, and ticks.
